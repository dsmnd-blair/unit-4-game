Weapons Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four weapons below. By clicking on a weapon, you will add a specific amount of points to your total. You win the game by matching your total to the random number, you lose by going above the number. The value of each weapon is hidden to you until you click on it. Every time the game is started, the value of the weapons change.
